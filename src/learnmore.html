The Tuscarora Athletic Ambassadors Club is organized to encourage and recognize volunteerism for Tuscarora athletic events. The TAAC will engage volunteers in productive tasks at the athletic concession stands, ticket booths, track meets, tailgate, senior picnic and other events. The TAAC will reward volulnteerism using a point base system to provide incentives, such as: t-shirts, volunteerism letters, event admittance and concession foods.

Point Awards, examples:

2 points -- Concessions Volunteer - whole night
1 point  -- Concessions Volunteer - one shift

2 points -- Tailgate Volunteer - all day
1 point  -- Tailgate Volunteer - one shift

2 points -- Track Meet Assistant - all day
1 point  -- Cross Country course set up
1 point  -- Cross Country  meet clean up

1 point  -- Event photographer

 

and others, as needed

 

 Point Reward, examples:

Yearly:
10 points -- T shirt
1 point = 1 dollar ; to purchase Concession Food and Gate Entrance
Gift Certificates for Top Contributors & Random Drawing

Lifetime:
40 points -- Letterman recognition
60 points -- Excellence Pin
80 points -- Excellence Star

100 points -- Excellence Star

50 points + Graduating Senior -- Robe Cord
50 points + Graduating Senior + Executive Board Member -- TBD

**It is possible that Yearly Points could be higher than Liftetime Points. Lifetime points are earned points only.
